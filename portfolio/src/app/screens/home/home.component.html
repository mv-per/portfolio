What started as a necessity soon turned into a genuine passion. I became captivated by the power of code to solve complex problems and automate repetitive tasks. Programming became an integral part of my daily routine as I delved into various languages and frameworks to expand my knowledge and skills.

Experiences

Throughout my programming journey, I have had the opportunity to work on a range of exciting projects. From developing software solutions to automate data analysis in chemical processes to creating intuitive user interfaces for real-time monitoring systems, each project has been a testament to my commitment and enthusiasm for programming. One notable accomplishment is the pyPTA. This project allowed me to combine my deep understanding of chemical engineering principles with my programming expertise, resulting in a successful automation solution that significantly improved efficiency and reduced time costs in simulating multicomponent adsorption.
